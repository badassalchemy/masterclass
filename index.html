Who's On The Other End Of This Call

Hi, I'm Danielle. I used to be where you are.

I came up inside an intentional community. Hippies, healers, the whole world. Energy was the only currency anyone believed in. My ex boyfriend told me money was evil and capitalistic — he'd shame me for every dollar I spent and every dollar I made.

The community wasn't better. They'd put me in a cage of language: you have enough, you work too much, you need to surrender more. I was being quietly brainwashed into staying small and calling it spirituality.

On the other side, my parents: "You need to make more money. You need to be a millionaire." One side telling me ambition was a sickness. The other side telling me my worth was a number. I was pinned between two narratives and neither one was mine.

So I detoxed both. I built my own relationship to money from scratch — and the year I finally left that community, I made my first six figures. That wasn't an accident. That was the cost of reclaiming my own mind.

Today I run a multi-six-figure agency that's helped founders raise $180M+ in startup capital and over $300M in real estate. I'm on calls with investors weekly. My day job is helping people build empires.

And the work I do with women? It's the work I had to do on myself first — to stop letting other people's stories about money, ambition, and worth run my life. Spirituality without strategy is just vibes. And vibes don't build the life you actually want.

— Danielle
